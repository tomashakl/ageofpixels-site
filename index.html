About

I am an enthusiast of the 80s/90s PC era, Amiga, and MIDI modules. After years of keeping things in storage, I now restore, record, and share them so they don’t get lost in drawers.

On YouTube, you’ll find authentic sound recordings from Roland MT-32, SC-55, and other cards/modules. On eBay/Aukro I regularly offer big box games, hardware, and hi-fi pieces from my collection.

My goal is to keep this era alive and accessible. Thanks for subscribing and following!
